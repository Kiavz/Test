Northanger Abbey

"Northanger Abbey" is a gothic and coming-of-age novel, which was written by Jane Austen. It was published back in 1817.

Summary

Catherine becomes friends with Isabella Thorpe, a charming person, and her brother, James Thorpe. She had also captured the eye of Henry Tilney, a wealthy and handsome man. Catherine gets invited to visit the Tilney family's estate, Northanger Abbey. Her Gothic novel-inspired imagination makes her imagine dark secrets and mysteries about the ancient Abbey, though her theories and expectations are far from reality. She learns from the consequences of letting her imagination lead her mind.

But throughout the story, she finds the complexities of love and friendships. She then gains a more mature understanding of the world and the people surrounding her, finding happiness and self-discovery.
